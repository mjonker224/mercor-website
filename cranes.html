FAQ

What information is needed for a crane hire quote?

Yes. Mercor Cranes can assist with loading, offloading and positioning depending on the load, location and site conditions.

Do you work outside Potchefstroom?

Yes. Work areas include Potchefstroom, North West, Gauteng, Free State and surrounding regions depending on job requirements.

Can you help with machinery moving?

Yes. Send the machinery type, mass, dimensions, photos and site access details so the correct lifting approach can be checked.
