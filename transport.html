FAQ

Common transport quote questions.

What information is needed for an abnormal load quote?

Load description, mass, dimensions, loading point, offloading point, route, countries, required date, photos and any loading/offloading requirements.

Do you handle cross-border abnormal transport?

Mercor Transport focuses on abnormal loads across Africa, subject to route, permit, border and project requirements.

Why do you need photos before quoting?

Photos help assess the load shape, lifting points, access, ground conditions and whether loading or offloading equipment may be required.

Can transport and crane support be combined?

Yes. Where needed, transport planning can be coordinated with crane, rigging, loading or offloading support.
